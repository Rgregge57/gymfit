ABOUT US

LADIES ONLY EXPERIENCE

Gymfit is a fitness studio/female society in Belfast that serves women only. Built and operated by women, too. Aside from the laid-back, friendly atmosphere and approachable woman at the front desk.

Gymfit was founded after a discussion at a coffee morning were we discovered many women had suffered negative experiences in male dominated gyms. We seek to provide that safe workout space without the hassle of being interupted by male gym goers.
